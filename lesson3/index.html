Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide up-to-date, area-specific weather information; as well as updates on local community events, places to visit and other related information.

A secondary purpose is to provide a place for those who live locally to connect, meet up and assist each other in locally-driven community initiatives. This will be done by creating a notice board/forum wear people can communicate with others in their area.
