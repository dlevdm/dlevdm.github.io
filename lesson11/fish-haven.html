Fish Haven

If you haven't heard of Fish Haven, Idaho that could be because, officially or on paper, it does not exist. Fish Haven is an unincorporated community along the shores of Bear Lake in Bear Lake County.

The Best Time to Visit

The closest city to Fish Haven is Paris, Idaho. WeatherSpark.com says that based on the tourism score, the best time of year to visit Paris (and, of course, Fish Haven) for warm-weather activities is from late June to mid August.
